About Me

Hello! I'm Katlynn, a cybersecurity graduate with a passion for building secure systems that work—and defend. My experience spans designing network infrastructure, evaluating security policies, and competing in hands-on cyber competitions like the National Cyber League.

After earning my Associate of Applied Science in Cybersecurity from Clark State College, I advanced my technical abilities through lab simulations, Packet Tracer builds, and red/blue team challenges. I am currently pursuing my bachelor's in Cybersecurity at Franklin University, further deepening my expertise in areas such as access control, threat analysis, and incident response.

My academic and professional accomplishments have earned me invitations to Phi Theta Kappa and Epsilon Pi Tau honor societies. I take pride in being both a meticulous technician and an effective communicator—someone who can both secure networks and explain the “why” behind the fix.
